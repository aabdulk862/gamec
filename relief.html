GAMEC Charity

Our Commitment:

GAMEC Charity stands as a beacon of compassion, dedicated to extending aid to those in need within the Muslim Eritrean community and beyond. Rooted in our mission to serve Allah (swt) through service to humanity, we endeavor to alleviate suffering and uphold dignity in every endeavor.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual, regardless of background, finds solace and support in times of adversity. GAMEC Charity aims to carve a path towards this vision, becoming synonymous with hope, resilience, and unwavering compassion.

Our Objectives:

  • Empowering Communities: We're committed to empowering marginalized Muslim Eritreans and individuals through tailored social service programs, fostering self-sufficiency and resilience.
  • Strengthening Bonds: We believe in nurturing strong family bonds by providing educational resources and services rooted in Islamic values, nurturing a sense of unity and belonging.
  • Forging Partnerships: Collaboration lies at the heart of our approach. We seek to forge meaningful partnerships with Islamic institutions, community organizations, and governmental agencies to amplify our impact and reach.
