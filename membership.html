Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join GAMEC?

Any Muslim Eritrean — or anyone who supports our mission — is welcome to join, regardless of where you live. We have members across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

What programs is GAMEC planning?

We're building programs for every generation: GAMEC Youth, GAMEC Sisters, GAMEC Seniors, GAMEC Professionals, health services, relief and charity work, and matrimonial services. These are in development — visit our programs page to learn more about what's coming.

How can I volunteer or contribute beyond membership?

We're always looking for volunteers — whether it's helping at events, mentoring youth, or lending professional skills. Reach out through our contact page and we'll connect you with the right program.

How do I stay updated on GAMEC news and events?

Follow us on Facebook, join our WhatsApp community groups, or simply sign up as a member to receive updates directly.
