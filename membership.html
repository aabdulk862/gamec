Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a membership fee?

GAMEC membership is free. We welcome voluntary donations to support our programs, but membership itself has no cost.

Who can join GAMEC?

Any Muslim Eritrean — or anyone who supports our mission — is welcome to join, regardless of where you live in the world.

How do I sign up?

Click the "Join Now" button below. You'll be taken to a short registration form that takes about 2 minutes to complete.

Is GAMEC a registered non-profit?

Yes. GAMEC Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization based in Washington, DC.
