Membership

Join the Global Association of Muslim Eritrean Communities (GAMEC) and become an integral part of a diverse and vibrant community dedicated to fostering unity, empowerment, and positive change. As a member, you gain access to a range of exclusive benefits that enrich your life and contribute to the advancement of our community's collective goals.

As an GAMEC member, you have the opportunity to:

  • Help support the vision, mission, and programs of GAMEC
  • Support GAMEC's programs and services as we:
    • Advance a positive image of Islam to the general society
    • Communicate needs and concerns of Eritrean Muslims to government agencies and officials
    • Empower youth to become future leaders in our community.
