GAMEC Health Services

GAMEC Health Services is committed to providing essential healthcare support to Muslim Eritrean communities. Our services include medical assistance, health education, and wellness programs designed to promote physical and mental well-being. We collaborate with local healthcare providers and volunteers to ensure access to quality care for all members of the community, regardless of their circumstances.

Our Health Services Include:

  • Medical Assistance: Access to healthcare professionals and medical resources.
  • Health Education: Workshops and seminars on various health topics.
  • Wellness Programs: Initiatives to promote physical and mental well-being.
  • Community Support: Assistance for those with chronic conditions or disabilities.
