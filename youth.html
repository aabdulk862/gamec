GAMEC Youth

GAMEC Youth strives to cultivate spiritually grounded, culturally proud, and socially responsible Muslim Eritrean youth. We empower young people through faith-based education, leadership programs, cultural engagement, and community service—helping them grow into confident and active contributors to society.

Our Mission

In The Short Term, we will provide accessible Islamic education, cultural activities, and social programs that foster brotherhood, understanding, and active participation among youth.

In the Long term, we will empower Muslim Eritrean youth through faith-based education, leadership development, cultural preservation, and community service, enabling them to become positive agents of change locally and globally.

Our Programs

  • Youth Islamic Skills Workshops: Quran, Hadith, and Fiqh sessions to build religious knowledge.
  • Cultural Celebrations: Eid events, Ramadan Iftar, and picnics.
  • Hosting Public Figures: Inviting community leaders and speakers to inspire and engage youth.
  • Sports & Recreation Days: Activities like soccer and camping to build health and camaraderie.
  • Community Service: Food drives and charity projects to foster civic responsibility.

Encouragement: With effort and guidance, youth can secure the support they need to pursue meaningful, faith-aligned academic journeys and emerge as leaders in their communities.
