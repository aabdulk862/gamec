GAMEC Youth

Empowering the Next Generation

The GAMEC Youth Committee is here to help you level up. We focus on building real connections, sharpening your skills, and creating safe spaces where you can lead, succeed, and own your future.

From academic support to community vibes, we provide the tools — guides, training, and events — to help you thrive.

Our Programs

Sports & Adventure

  • Team Sports: Soccer, basketball, volleyball, or baseball to build discipline and teamwork.
  • Individual Sports: Swimming, tennis, martial arts, or gymnastics.
  • Outdoor Adventures: Hiking, biking, and camping.

Arts & Creative Expression

  • Theater & Drama: Build confidence and find your voice on stage.
  • Visual Arts: Drawing, photography, or sculpture workshops.
  • Dance: Traditional dance connecting with heritage.

STEM & Academic Excellence

  • Coding & Robotics: Hands-on tech programs.
  • Quran Recitation: Live Zoom competitions.
  • Math & Science: Annual challenges and real-world problems.
  • Poetry & Writing: Original poems and translations at GAMEC events.

Leadership & Personal Growth

  • Mentorship: Career and life advice, virtual or in-person.
  • Youth Councils: Make real decisions for the community.
  • Debate Club: Persuasion and public speaking.

Service & Social Impact

  • Charity & Relief: Lead fundraising efforts globally.
  • Social Justice: Advocate for marginalized communities through expert-led training.

Culture & Connection

  • Cultural Exchange: Connect with Muslim Eritrean youth worldwide.
  • Social Meetups: Safe spaces and Matrimonial Services.
  • Public Speaking: Grab attention and close with impact.

Career & Future Skills

  • Entrepreneurship: Launch your own business or manage big projects.
  • Internships: Hands-on experience with GAMEC professionals.
  • Financial Literacy: Budgeting, saving, and investing.

Tutoring & Life Skills

  • Flexible Tutoring: Give back 5–10 hours a week on your schedule.
  • Life Skill Workshops: Transferable skills for daily life and career.
  • Sustainability: Lead eco-friendly projects.
