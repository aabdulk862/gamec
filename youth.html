GAMEC Youth

GAMEC Youth is committed to empowering young Muslim Eritreans through education, mentorship, and community engagement. Our mission is to provide a supportive environment where youth can connect, collaborate, and thrive. We prioritize establishing educational programs, offering mentorship opportunities, organizing community service initiatives, and fostering unity among Muslim youth. Embracing diversity, heritage, and shared values, we strive to build a more united and resilient Muslim Eritrean community. Join us in shaping a brighter future for our youth and community.
