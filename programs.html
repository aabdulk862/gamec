GAMEC Programs

Explore the programs the Global Association of Muslim Eritrean Communities (GAMEC) is developing. Our planned initiatives will empower, educate, and foster unity within our community for individuals of all ages and interests.

Programs we're building for individuals of all ages and backgrounds:

  • GAMEC Charity

    Planned to extend aid to those in need within the Muslim Eritrean community and beyond.

  • GAMEC Sisters

    Dedicated to empowering women within the community.

  • GAMEC Youth

    Focused on engaging and nurturing the youth demographic.

  • GAMEC Seniors

    Designed to support and enrich the lives of senior members.

  • GAMEC Professionals

    Aimed at fostering professional development and networking opportunities.
