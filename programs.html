GAMEC Programs

Discover the diverse and enriching programs offered by the Global Association of Muslim Eritrean Communities (GAMEC). Our initiatives empower, educate, and foster unity within our community for individuals of all ages and interests.

Our programs serve individuals of all ages and backgrounds:

  • GAMEC Relief

    Extending aid to those in need within the Muslim Eritrean community and beyond.

  • GAMEC Sisters

    Empowering women within the community.

  • GAMEC Youth

    Engaging and nurturing the youth demographic.

  • GAMEC Seniors

    Supporting and enriching the lives of senior members.

  • GAMEC Professionals

    Fostering professional development and networking opportunities.
