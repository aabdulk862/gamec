GAMEC Programs

The Global Association of Muslim Eritrean Communities is developing programs to empower, educate, and foster unity within our community. These initiatives serve individuals of all ages and backgrounds.

Core Programs

Each program addresses a specific need within our community, from charitable relief to professional development.

GAMEC Charity

Extending aid to those in need within the Muslim Eritrean community and beyond, rooted in our mission to serve through compassion.

GAMEC Sisters

Empowering women through Islamic education, leadership development, and community engagement across ages and backgrounds.

GAMEC Youth

Nurturing spiritually grounded, culturally proud, and socially responsible young people through education, sports, and cultural activities.

GAMEC Seniors

Supporting senior members with compassionate care, community connections, and resources to maintain independence and dignity.

GAMEC Professionals

Connecting Muslim Eritrean professionals across industries with networking, mentorship, and career development opportunities.

Health Services

Providing essential healthcare support including medical assistance, health education, wellness programs, and community support.
