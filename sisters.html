GAMEC Sisters

The GAMEC Sisters program, a vital initiative within GAMEC, focuses on empowering women of all backgrounds through Islamic education and support. Our mission is to provide platforms for sisters to explore and strengthen their faith within their communities. We aim to represent the diversity of Muslim women around the world, fostering inclusivity across ages, genders, ethnicities, and economic backgrounds. By expanding membership and embracing diversity, we strive to become a leading institution, utilizing technology and collaboration to support our vision of spiritual growth and community engagement among sisters.
