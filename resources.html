Resources

Welcome to the Resources page of the Global Association of Muslim Eritrean Communities (GAMEC). Here, we have curated a collection of valuable resources to empower and enrich our community members. These resources cover a wide range of topics, from educational materials and cultural content to social welfare resources and interfaith materials.

Islamic Resources

  • Quran.com - Read, listen to, and study the Quran online.
  • Sunnah - Access to authentic Hadith collections in English.
  • Prayer Times - Accurate prayer times for locations worldwide.
  • Mosque Directory - Find mosques and Islamic centers around the world.
  • Islamic Relief - Charity organization providing humanitarian aid and development.

Educational Resources

  • WhyIslam - Information about Islam and Islamic practices.
  • IslamiCity - Educational articles, videos, and resources on Islam.
  • Muslim Heritage - Provides resources on Islamic contributions to global civilization.
