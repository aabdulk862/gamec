Donate

Your support fuels our mission and makes a tangible difference in the lives of Muslim Eritreans inside Eritrea and communities worldwide. By donating to the Global Association of Muslim Eritrean Communities (GAMEC), you contribute to initiatives that uplift, empower, and unite our community. Every contribution, no matter the size, is a step towards positive change and greater opportunities for individuals and families within our community.
