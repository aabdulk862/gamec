Mission and Vision

At AMEAC, our mission is to create a supportive and inclusive environment where Muslim Eritrean Americans can connect, collaborate, and thrive. We are committed to preserving our rich cultural heritage, nurturing strong Islamic values, and building bridges of understanding with the wider American society.

Learn More

Programs and Initiatives

Our programs and initiatives form the heart of AMEAC's dedication to uplifting our community. From engaging educational workshops and empowering social welfare projects to vibrant cultural events and enlightening interfaith dialogues, our diverse range of initiatives aims to inspire, educate, and unite.

Learn More

Get Involved

We invite you to be an integral part of AMEAC, where your passion and commitment can make a significant impact. Whether you wish to volunteer your time, support our endeavors through donations, or simply participate in our enriching events, your involvement can help shape a brighter future for Eritrean Muslims.
