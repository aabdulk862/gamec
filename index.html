Mission and Vision

GAMEC is a 501(c)(3) non-profit association dedicated to serving Allah (SWT) by uniting Muslim Eritreans worldwide in a supportive, inclusive community where we connect, collaborate, and thrive.

Donations

Your support fuels our mission and makes a tangible difference in the lives of Muslim Eritreans inside Eritrea and communities worldwide.
