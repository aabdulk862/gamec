Mission and Vision

GAMEC is a non-governmental, non-political, not-for-profit 501(c)(3) voluntary membership association composed of organizations, communities, villages, and individuals dedicated to being of service to Allah (SWT) and strengthening the global Muslim Eritrean community.

Programs and Initiatives

From faith-based education and community relief to youth development and professional networking, GAMEC offers programs serving Muslim Eritreans of every generation, including Sisters, Youth, Seniors, Professionals, health services, and charitable relief.
