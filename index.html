Mission and Vision

GAMEC is a non-governmental, non-political, not-for-profit 501(c)(3) voluntary membership association composed of organizations, communities, villages, and individuals dedicated to being of service to Allah (SWT) and strengthening the global Muslim Eritrean community.

Matrimonial Services

GAMEC's Matrimonial Services helps Muslim Eritrean community members find compatible life partners based on shared values, faith, and cultural backgrounds. Rooted in Islamic principles, our program provides a trusted and respectful platform for meaningful connections.
