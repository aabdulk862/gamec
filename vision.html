Mission and Vision

Who We Are

GAMEC is a leading Muslim Eritrean organization serving communities outside of Eritrea. We are a non-governmental, non-political, not-for-profit 501(c)(3) voluntary membership association composed of organizations, communities, villages, and individuals dedicated to being of service to Allah (SWT) and strengthening the global Muslim Eritrean community.

We are committed to advancing the social, civic, cultural, and spiritual fabric of Muslim Eritreans in North America and around the world, preserving our rich heritage, nurturing strong Islamic values, and promoting unity, leadership, and civic responsibility among our members.

Our Mission

To advance the social, civic, cultural, and spiritual well-being of Muslim Eritreans worldwide by preserving our heritage, nurturing Islamic values, and promoting unity, leadership, and civic responsibility.

Our Vision

A united, resilient, and harmonious global Muslim Eritrean community that fosters meaningful connections with the Eritrean motherland and empowers its members to thrive in democratic societies worldwide.

GAMEC will also foster meaningful connections with the Eritrean motherland in matters concerning the religious, civic, and social well-being of all Eritreans. It will educate community members about their rights and responsibilities within democratic societies while organizing and coordinating local chapters and regional bodies across various countries to expand its global network and further its mission.

Our Priorities

As concerned Muslim Eritreans, seniors and youth alike, we recognize these guiding priorities for our community.

  • Establish a faith-based civic association addressing social justice issues.
  • Unite Muslim Eritrean communities around a common mission.
  • Support the development of Islamic community centers globally.
  • Create dedicated Youth, Women, Senior, and Professional Associations.
  • Strengthen Islamic knowledge through connections with scholars and sheikhs.
  • Collaborate with other Islamic organizations for the greater good.
  • Provide relief and development assistance for Eritrea and refugee communities.
  • Organize interfaith dialogues and outreach initiatives.

"Diversity is a divine blessing, and as Muslims, working together toward a common goal (Islam) is the key to our success."

By embracing our heritage, values, and a shared commitment to positive change, we endeavor to build a more united, resilient, and harmonious Muslim Eritrean community.
