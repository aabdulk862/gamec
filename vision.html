Mission and Vision

GAMEC is a non-profit 501(c)(3) voluntary membership association of organisations individuals whoose mission is to be of service to Allah (swt). Our mission is to create a supportive and inclusive environment where Muslim Eritreans around the world can connect, collaborate, and thrive. We are committed to preserving our rich cultural heritage, nurturing strong Islamic values, and building bridges of understanding.

As concerned Muslim Eritreans (seniors and youth), we recognize the following priorities:

  • Establishing a faith-based (deen) civic association, with a focus on addressing social and econimic justice issues in general and particularly within Eritrea.
  • Uniting Muslim Eritrean communities under one Association for a shared mission and direction.
  • Helping current Muslim Eritrean communities establish islamic community centers.
  • Creating dedicated Islamic Youth, Women, Senior and Professional Associations.
  • Strengthening our Islamic knowledge by connecting with Muslim scholars and Eritrean sheikhs.
  • Connecting and working with other Islamic Organizations for the greater good of our deen.
  • Establishing a relief and development organization to help those in need inside Eritrea and Muslim refugees around the world.
  • Organizing dialoge events to show the need of interfaith dialog and out reach between Muslims and Non Muslims.

We believe that diversity is our blessing and working together is the key to our success.

By embracing our heritage, values, and a shared commitment to positive change, we endeavor to build a more united, resilient, and harmonious Muslim Eritrean community.
